Shine bright, my lanterns

I am a believer in true love, fairytales & wishing upon stars and found myself dreaming about the process of falling in deep love in 2018. Then, I started to write a song that summer about the butterflies that tickle your tummy before your heart floats away with them and you find yourself hopelessly/endlessly in love. I ran with that theme and kept writing more songs about every step of the way when you let yourself fall into another. I didn't expect to end up with a whole album of songs about all the steps of falling in love just 1 short year later! I'm still writing mostly uplifting and loving songs and can't wait to show you what's next! Connect with me on social media and let me know what you think of my songs. 🤗🏮
